About Us

Who are we?

We are a record label consisting of musicians and artists across the Local Group specializing in experimental music. We're influenced by the sounds of our universe; both wanted and unwanted.

No, but really: who are we?

We're glad you asked! My name is DeathNight, and I've been producing electronic music for about 4 years on and off. This project is a homebrew ran and created entirely by me.
IPNS is based on the sounds of reality- everything from the mundane to the endlessly complex. It was created for artists to share their messages, emotions and ideas without restriction.
We believe experimental sound and art is the best tool to capture one's emotions in the moment; unburded by restrictions in genre or format. There's no need to follow convention in these creations.
We're inspired by everything you could imagine. The world is full of meaning; you just need to find it.

Releasing on CDs, cassettes, and digital as affordable as we can make it, if not free. Check our Releases page for new music.

To release on IPNS, submit your demos to our Demos page.

We hope you like what you hear. Don't be afraid to say hi.
