DeathNight, also known as DeathNight77 is a Canadian music producer and electronist based out of Port Moody. Listening to dubstep on YouTube during his childhood inspired him to produce his own music starting in 2019. His music has evolved with him, transitioning through numerous genres and styles over the years. Prior to IPNS, he was briefly signed to LIM1TS, an Australian dubstep weblabel.

Outside of music, he is an avid outdoorsman, spending his time hiking, biking and orienteering. He is currently in the grade 12 year of his education.

Genres: Electronic, dubstep, noise, EBM
