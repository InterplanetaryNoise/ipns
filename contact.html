Demo Submission

Submit your music

You can submit anything you'd like- a single, an EP, an album, everything in between.
If you have album art, booklets, inserts, notes, documents, promotional material, bonus tracks or ANY additional elements you'd like to include, don't be shy- add those too!

Email

Submit your music via email to interplanetarynoisesociety@gmail.com. The subject should be "Demo". Please upload as a compressed archive (.zip, .rar, .7z, etc) with everything in it.

Include the following details:
- The name of your track/album/piece/etc - The tracklist of your piece
- What you would like to be released as (your pseudonym)
- Information about you as an artist (for examples, see the artists page- this is where you'll go!)
- What your real name is (optional, this is only if you want to appear in the "credits" with your real name instead of your pseudonym)
- The inspiration for your piece. This is optional, but we like to know what inspires our artists- plus, it makes it easier to create album art if none exists.
- Any special requests regarding the piece or the artwork, or the release (i.e on CDs or cassettes) (be as descriptive as needed)

From here, we will contact you back to negotiate pricing.

Form

If emailing is not an option, submit your music using this form.

Direct Upload

If you cannot use the form to submit, you can upload your work here directly as a compressed archive (.zip, .rar, .7z, etc) with everything in it. Provide contact information if you can. We will still try to release without it, but if there's an issue we may not be able to. Note without contact information, we cannot give you funds for your release and it will be priced free.



Include a file with the details mentioned in the Email section of this page. In case the button is broken, the link is here.

Other contact methods

If you cannot submit your demos with the above methods, you can message deathnightdubs on Discord.

Non-musical related inquiries

If you would like to contact IPNS for any other reason, direct all communication to either interplanetarynoisesociety@gmail.com or deathnightdubs on Discord. Don't be afraid to say hi!
